Frequently Asked Questions

I am a legislator/policymaker/researcher. How can I use STARA?

STARA is currently available in closed beta. We're excited to work with policymakers and researchers to use STARA to support their work. If you are are interested in carrying out a project with our system, please feel free to reach out to us at reglab [at] law [dot] stanford [dot] edu. We are not able to respond to all inbounds, but it is particularly helpful if you can let us know who you are, what jurisdiction you are interested in, and what kind of reform/research effort you'd like to undertake.
